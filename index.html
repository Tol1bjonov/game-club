Counter Strike 2

Counter-Strike 2 (CS2) is the latest installment in the iconic Counter-Strike series, built on the powerful Source 2 engine. Known for its tactical gameplay, CS2 offers improved graphics, physics, and map designs, alongside faster response times and smoother gameplay. Players engage in intense 5v5 matches as either terrorists or counter-terrorists, planting or defusing bombs, or rescuing hostages. With enhanced visuals, new maps, and a refreshed competitive scene, CS2 promises to deliver both nostalgia for long-time fans and excitement for newcomers.

Dota 2

Dota 2 is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed by Valve. Itâ€™s one of the most popular and competitive esports titles, featuring two teams of five players, each controlling a unique hero with distinct abilities. The goal is to destroy the enemyâ€™s Ancient, located in their base, while protecting your own. Dota 2 is known for its deep strategy, complex gameplay mechanics, and a vast roster of over 100 heroes. The game offers regular updates, new heroes, and international tournaments, such as The International, where top teams compete for millions in prize money.

Volarant

Valorant is a tactical first-person shooter (FPS) developed by Riot Games. Released in 2020, the game blends strategic, team-based gameplay with precise shooting mechanics. Players select from a diverse roster of "Agents," each with unique abilities that enhance gameplay beyond traditional gunplay. Teams of five face off in objective-based matches, typically involving planting/defusing bombs or eliminating the opposing team. Valorant's emphasis on teamwork, strategy, and gun mechanics has made it a highly popular title in the esports scene, with competitive play and regular updates keeping the community engaged.

The Sims 4

The Sims 4 is a life simulation video game developed by Maxis and published by Electronic Arts. Released in 2014, it's part of The Sims series, where players create and control virtual characters (Sims) as they live their everyday lives. Players can design homes, form relationships, pursue careers, and explore various life events like marriage, parenthood, and aging. The game features customizable Sims, unique personalities, and a wide variety of expansion packs and downloadable content that add new features, locations, and gameplay options. The Sims 4 has a huge community, with players frequently sharing their creations and stories online.

Overwatch 2

Overwatch 2 is a team-based first-person shooter developed by Blizzard Entertainment and released in 2022 as the sequel to the popular game Overwatch (2016). It introduces new maps, heroes, and game modes while evolving the gameplay to focus on 5v5 matches instead of 6v6. Overwatch 2 places an emphasis on hero progression, with each character having distinct abilities and playstyles. The game also features a new "Push" mode, where teams race to control a robot and push it toward the enemy's side. Overwatch 2 has a free-to-play model with a seasonal battle pass system for unlocking skins, emotes, and other cosmetics. The game continues to evolve with regular updates and events.

Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world action role-playing game developed by CD Projekt Red, released in December 2020. Set in the dystopian Night City, the game immerses players in a futuristic world filled with advanced technology, cybernetic enhancements, and rampant crime. Players take on the role of V, a customizable character seeking to uncover the truth behind a mysterious implant that holds the digital ghost of a legendary rockerboy named Johnny Silverhand, voiced by Keanu Reeves. The game features a dynamic narrative, multiple playstyles, and expansive exploration, with a focus on combat, hacking, and dialogue choices. Despite a rocky launch, Cyberpunk 2077 has received significant updates and improvements, making it a more refined experience.

The witcher 3

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is an action role-playing game developed by CD Projekt Red, released in May 2015. It is the third installment in The Witcher series, based on the book series by Andrzej Sapkowski. Players control Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter known as a Witcher, as he embarks on a quest to find his adopted daughter, Ciri, who is being hunted by the mysterious Wild Hunt. The game is set in a vast, open world, rich with detailed environments, complex characters, and branching narratives that offer multiple endings. It features a mix of combat, exploration, and decision-making, where choices made throughout the game have significant consequences. Widely regarded as one of the greatest video games of all time, The Witcher 3 received numerous awards for its storytelling, world-building, and gameplay mechanics. It also had two major expansions, Hearts of Stone and Blood and Wine, which added even more content and depth to the game.

Elden Ring

Elden Ring is an action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment, released in February 2022. The game is directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki, the creator of the Dark Souls series, and features collaboration with A Song of Ice and Fire author George R. R. Martin, who helped craft the game's lore and world-building. Set in the vast, open world of the Lands Between, Elden Ring allows players to explore a fractured, mysterious world filled with challenging enemies, powerful bosses, and deep, interconnected lore. Players control the Tarnished, a character tasked with seeking the shattered Elden Ring to become the Elden Lord. The game is known for its intricate world design, punishing difficulty, and freedom of exploration, encouraging players to venture off the beaten path. With a rich fantasy setting, dynamic combat, and numerous character-building options, Elden Ring has received widespread acclaim for its storytelling, gameplay, and immersive open-world experience. It's considered one of the best games of the 2020s and has won multiple Game of the Year awards.

Subnautica

Subnautica is an open-world survival action-adventure game developed and published by Unknown Worlds Entertainment. Released in early access in 2014 and fully launched in 2018, the game is set in the distant future, where players explore an alien underwater world after their spaceship, the Aurora, crashes on an ocean-covered planet. The goal of Subnautica is to survive, explore, and eventually escape the planet by collecting resources, crafting tools, and building habitats. Players dive into the vast, beautifully rendered oceans, uncovering mysterious environments filled with dangerous creatures, hidden secrets, and valuable resources. As they explore deeper, they face ever-increasing dangers, from hostile sea creatures to the threat of dehydration and hunger. The game features a rich story that unravels through exploration, with players discovering logs, data pads, and artifacts left behind by other explorers. It has been praised for its atmospheric world-building, engaging gameplay mechanics, and stunning underwater visuals. Subnautica also has a sequel, Subnautica: Below Zero, which explores new regions and introduces new survival challenges.

Life is Strange

Life is Strange is an episodic graphic adventure game developed by Dontnod Entertainment and published by Square Enix. Originally released in 2015, it follows the story of Max Caulfield, a photography student at Blackwell Academy, who discovers she has the ability to rewind time. The game is set in the fictional town of Arcadia Bay, Oregon, and centers on Max's efforts to navigate the complexities of friendship, love, and the consequences of her newfound powers. The narrative unfolds over five episodes, where players make decisions that shape the story, and the consequences of these choices are often reflected later in the game. Throughout the journey, Max tries to uncover the mysteries surrounding her town, including a missing student and strange occurrences that may be tied to her powers. The game is known for its emotional depth, dealing with themes like mental health, identity, and the impact of choices. Life is Strange received widespread acclaim for its storytelling, character development, and the way it handles difficult, real-world issues. Its success led to the release of prequels and sequels, including Life is Strange: Before the Storm and Life is Strange 2. A remastered version was also released in 2022, bringing updated graphics and gameplay improvements.

Disco Elysium

Disco Elysium is a critically acclaimed role-playing game developed by ZA/UM. Released in 2019, it follows a troubled detective with amnesia as he investigates a murder in a dystopian city. The game focuses on dialogue, decision-making, and character development rather than combat. Players shape the protagonist's personality through choices, with various skills and traits influencing interactions and outcomes. Known for its deep narrative, philosophical themes, and unique art style, Disco Elysium won numerous awards and is celebrated for its complex storytelling and character-driven gameplay.

Sekiro

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice is an action-adventure game developed by FromSoftware and released in 2019. Set in a reimagined late 1500s Japan, players control a shinobi named Wolf on a quest to rescue his kidnapped lord and seek revenge on his enemies. The game emphasizes precise combat, stealth, and exploration. Unlike FromSoftware's other titles, Sekiro focuses on a more aggressive, skill-based combat system with a strong emphasis on timing and parrying. Its challenging difficulty, rich storytelling, and stunning visuals earned it critical acclaim, winning Game of the Year at The Game Awards 2019.

Fifa 23

FIFA 23 is the latest installment in the popular football simulation video game series developed by EA Sports. Released in 2022, it marks the final game in the long-standing partnership between EA and FIFA. Featuring realistic gameplay, enhanced graphics, and a vast roster of teams, players, and leagues, FIFA 23 offers both single-player and multiplayer modes, including career mode, ultimate team, and online play. The game introduces new mechanics like HyperMotion2 technology, enhancing player movements and animations. It also features the menâ€™s and womenâ€™s World Cups, making it a celebration of global football.

Football manager

Football Manager is a highly acclaimed sports simulation video game series developed by Sports Interactive. It allows players to take on the role of a football team manager, making decisions about tactics, transfers, training, and more. The game features real-life leagues, teams, and players from around the world, offering a highly detailed and immersive experience. Players can guide their chosen club to success, balancing both on-field strategies and off-field management. With its deep mechanics and extensive database, Football Manager is a favorite among football enthusiasts who enjoy the challenge of managing a club's day-to-day operations.

Pes(eFootball)

PES (Pro Evolution Soccer) / eFootball is a popular football video game series developed by Konami. It focuses on delivering realistic gameplay, with a strong emphasis on fluid player movements, tactical depth, and accurate ball physics. Originally known as Pro Evolution Soccer, it was rebranded to eFootball starting in 2021, transitioning to a free-to-play model. The game features licensed teams, stadiums, and players, although it is known for having fewer official licenses compared to its competitor FIFA. PES/eFootball is beloved for its tactical realism and precision gameplay, attracting fans who appreciate a more simulation-oriented football experience.

NBA 2k24

NBA 2K24 is the latest installment in the NBA 2K series, developed by Visual Concepts and published by 2K Sports. It offers a highly immersive basketball experience with realistic graphics, gameplay, and player movements. The game features both single-player and multiplayer modes, including the popular MyCareer, MyTeam, and online play. NBA 2K24 includes updated rosters, new player animations, and improved AI. The game also introduces new features, such as expanded career modes, enhanced customization options, and better online connectivity. It is widely regarded for its attention to detail and depth, making it a go-to choice for basketball fans.

Riders Republic

Riders Republic is an extreme sports video game developed by Ubisoft Annecy. It allows players to participate in various thrilling outdoor sports, including skiing, snowboarding, mountain biking, and wingsuiting, all within an open-world, multiplayer environment. Set in iconic national parks across the U.S., the game offers a massive playground where players can team up or compete in chaotic, large-scale events. With a focus on fun and freedom, Riders Republic blends realistic sports mechanics with an over-the-top, action-packed atmosphere. The game also features character customization, progression systems, and a vibrant social hub for players to interact.

Steep

Steep is an open-world extreme sports game developed by Ubisoft Annecy. Set in the snowy mountains of the Alps and Alaska, the game lets players experience a variety of winter sports, including skiing, snowboarding, wingsuit flying, and paragliding. Players can freely explore vast landscapes, complete challenges, and take part in online competitions. The game emphasizes fluid movement, breathtaking views, and the freedom to tackle mountains in creative ways. With a focus on exploration and adrenaline-fueled gameplay, Steep offers a unique winter sports experience with a social and competitive edge.
